Airborne School at a Glance

  1. Ground Week
    • In-process
    • Morning PT (daily)
    • Learn how to wear the parachute
    • Learn exiting the aircraft procedures
    • Jump from the 34-foot tower
  2. Tower Week
    • Morning PT (daily)
    • Jump from the 34-foot tower with a combat load
    • Practice the PLF (parachute landing fall)
    • Practice the PLF some more...
    • Utilize the swing landing trainer
  3. Jump Week
    • Run to the airstrip
    • Sit in the harness shed for hours and hours on end
    • Jump from a high-performance aircraft 5 times:
      1. Hollywood day
      2. Hollywood day
      3. Hollywood night
      4. Combat day
      5. Hollywood day
    • Graduation day
